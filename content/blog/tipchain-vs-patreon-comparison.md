## 1. Fees & Economics: The Real Cost of "Free"

### Patreon

**Official Fee Structure:**
- Lite: 5% platform fee
- Pro: 8% platform fee
- Premium: 12% platform fee

**Hidden Costs:**
- Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Currency conversion: 2.5%
- Payout fees: $0.25 per payout (or 1% for PayPal)
- VAT collection: Variable by region

**Real Example:**
A European creator earning $1,000 from 100 U.S. supporters:
- Platform fee (Pro tier): $80
- Payment processing: ~$32
- Currency conversion: $25
- Payout fee: ~$10
- **Total fees: $147 (14.7%)**
- **You keep: $853**

For smaller creators below $1,000/month, effective fees often exceed 20%.

### Ko-fi

**Official Fee Structure:**
- Free tier: 0% (but with payment processing fees)
- Ko-fi Gold: $6/month subscription

**Hidden Costs:**
- Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 (via Stripe/PayPal)
- Currency conversion: 2.5-3.5%
- Instant payouts: Additional 1% (optional)
- Premium features: Behind paywall

**Real Example:**
Same $1,000 from 100 supporters:
- Platform fee: $0
- Ko-fi Gold subscription: $6
- Payment processing: ~$32
- Currency conversion: ~$30
- **Total fees: $68 (6.8%)**
- **You keep: $932**

Significantly better than Patreon, but payment processing and conversion fees still compound.

### TipChain

**Official Fee Structure:**
- Platform fee: 0%
- Gas fees: Sponsored (0% for users)
- Network fees: Absorbed by protocol

**Real Costs:**
- Blockchain transaction: $0 (sponsored)
- Currency conversion: Optional (receive native crypto)
- Withdrawal to fiat: 0.5-1% via decentralized exchanges (optional)

**Real Example:**
Same $1,000 from 100 supporters in USDC stablecoin:
- Platform fee: $0
- Gas fees: $0 (sponsored)
- Receive crypto directly: $0
- Optional conversion to fiat: ~$10
- **Total fees: $10 (1%)**
- **You keep: $990**

**The Verdict: TipChain saves creators 85-93% on fees.**

## 2. Payout Speed: Cash Flow Is King

### Patreon

**Payout Schedule:**
- Monthly, typically between 5th-10th of month
- First payout: After minimum $10 threshold
- Processing time: 7-14 business days

**Reality Check:**
Tips received January 15th hit your bank account around February 15-20th. That's **30-35 days** from tip to available funds.

Ko-fi offers the best Web2 payout experience. Tips can be in your PayPal within minutes, though bank transfers still take several days. Monthly threshold is only $5.

Tips received today → PayPal today (with 1% instant fee) or next business day (free).

- Blockchain settlement: 10-60 seconds
- Available in wallet: Immediately after confirmation
- Withdrawal to fiat: 1-2 hours via DEX, 24 hours via exchange
- No minimum threshold

**Reality Check:**
Tips received at 2 PM are in your wallet by 2:01 PM. If you need fiat, convert to USDC and cash out via Coinbase within hours—not weeks.

For international creators, this eliminates:
- Multi-day international wire transfers
- Currency conversion delays
- Weekend/holiday banking closures
